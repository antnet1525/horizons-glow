My Letter

Dear Jeanette,

Look into the night sky when you get a chance. The moon is beautiful tonight, isn't it?

But isn't the darkness beautiful too?

The night sky is expansive: oftentimes a sea of pure black, speckled by stars and illuminated by the moon.
We may be separated by thousands of miles, yet somehow we're closer than we have ever been before. When I look up at the sky, it feels like my future: vast, opaque, and uncertain.

And still, I always search for the moon. Not merely as an excuse to tell you it is beautiful tonight, but maybe because I look for you.
No, you are not the moon. You are not actually visible in this webpage. And yet, your prescence can be felt in everything on this page.
The night sky holds everything quietly in its embrace. Somewhere within that canvas, I look for you; you stare up at the same sky.

Before I bring color into this world, I wanted this to be our place to start. Before we phase through the days until I get to see you, I wanted to display my hidden passions.
The black isn't emptiness, it's the depth of my feelings and commitment. As we grow towards our unknown future, the gravity of our love will hold us together to engulf the vastness before us.

Love,
Anthony
